Help

Profile

Here you can find all your personal information. You can also delete your profile by clicking the red button "Delete profile".

Scan

Enter the url you want to check in the input. Click on the blue "scan" button and wait for the results. Your project is now in the projects list.

Projects

Here you will find all your scanned projects. In the search bar you can go through project search name. If you click on the blue button with "Details" you will see the errors automatically updated. You can delete it by clicking the red button with the trash can projects.

Feedback

We strive for satisfied customers of yours and ours. If there is something you do not understand, please enter your name, email, subject and message. Click the blue "Send" button and you will receive a reply from us to the email you entered.

Log Out

Clicking on this will ask you if you really want to explain. It is important that when you log out all your data is stored securely. The red button with "log out" takes you to the homepage and the green button with "No" leaves the popup disappear.
